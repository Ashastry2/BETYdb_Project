Help Page

Using this page, you can perform a serach in two ways. You can either look up the species that have a particular trait or you can look up all the traits pertaining to a species. The first species search will return all the traits of that species and a histogram of mean values collected for all of the traits. Similarly, using the traits search, you can display all the species as a table, accompanied by a histogram of mean value measurement for all the species with that trait.

PFT page displays the Plant functional trait information for 6 differnt PFTs. This is downloaded from TRY database.

Sites/Yields page gives species and traits information on a geological scale. If you click on the location displyed listed in the table, the page will display all the species in that region alogn with the traits and their mean trait value.
